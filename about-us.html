About Us

Established in December 2021, Royal Canal BJJ is owned and operated by the black belt husband and wife team of John and Jane Smith. Royal Canal BJJ caters for adults of all skill sets and ages (and coming soon, kids classes, WATCH THIS SPACE!!!), where everyone can learn and train in a fun and safe environment.


At our gym, our priority is providing world class coaching in a fun environment. We offer a broad schedule of classes to suit your busy lives and our doors are always open to new faces.


Whether you’re a beginner trying out a new hobby or a competitive grappler looking for the edge in your next competition, Royal Canal BJJ is the place for you.


Come make new friends, get in shape, and boost your self-confidence. We look forward to seeing you soon!


Our Coaches

John Smith

John is a 2nd Degree Brazilian Jiu Jitsu black belt under Roger Gracie with over 20 years’ experience grappling and coaching. He has lived and competed all over the world including Europe, South America, and North America. John holds multiple EBI, IBJJF, and Pan-European Titles, as well as competing in multiple times at ADCC. After years travelling and fighting around the world, the call of home finally brought him back to Ireland. While John hasn’t missed the weather, he has missed the Guinness!!


Jane Smith

Jane is a 2nd Degree Brazilian Jiu Jitsu black belt under Roger Gracie with over 18 years’ experience grappling and coaching. As with John, Jane has also lived and competed all over the world, holding multiple National, European, and World titles, both in the gi and nogi. When Jane is not lifting gold and standing on podiums, she enjoys kayaking and mountain climbing.
